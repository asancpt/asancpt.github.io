## Conversion

- Download `imagemagick` <http://www.imagemagick.org/script/download.php>

```bash
# montage.sh
rm $1*.png
convert -density 400 $1.pdf -resize 800x600 $1%03d.png
montage -mode concatenate -tile 4x $1*.png $1.png
open $1.png
```
